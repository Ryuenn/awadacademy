About the Program

A structured, attorney-led training designed to help you understand magistrate court procedures and approach your case with clarity and confidence.

Awad Academy

Program Overview

Awad Academy is a nonprofit organization dedicated to the science of educating trial lawyers in obtaining righteous verdicts for their clients. The program focuses on developing the strategies, skills, and courtroom mindset necessary for effective trial advocacy.

Through immersive instruction and real-world insights, Awad Academy provides educational training designed to help participants better understand trial preparation, jury engagement, and persuasive case presentation.
